Mission

Psychological disorders such as depression, anxiety disorder, bipolar disorder, and ADHD are widespread across the globe. In the United States, over 40 million people suffer from anxiety disorders alone.


PersonalSpace aims to improve the quality of life of early space travelers by focusing on:

  • Serving as a platform for monitoring mental health and promoting wellness
  • Providing them with a wide array of research-based remote intervention methods
